  • JQuery
  • Bootstrap

About the site

The development on this site was started in December 2016 and was completed in May 2017.

The Gordon Center located at UC San Diego contracted our web development team to help develop their site, and I was assigned to the task. The goal of the site is to provide students with a newly refreshed web design to better fit their needs. Certain elements were created by an external design firm, but I was tasked with converting the wireframe into a fully functional webpage.

The result of this new site is a fully responsive website that students could now view and browse on their phones, as well as on their computers.

The site uses Bootstrap as its responsive framework, and Coldfusion is used in order to communicate with the mailer module that the site is hosted on.
